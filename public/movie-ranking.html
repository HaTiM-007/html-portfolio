Best Movies According To Sandeep

My Top Three Movies

INTERSTELLAR

Directed by the great Christopher Nolan.One of the greatest sci-fi movies of all time, featuring a great cast including Mathew McConaughey,Jessica Chastain and anne hathaway in the lead.The story revolves around-a goup of astronauts trying to look for a new home as Earth is going to get uninhabitable soon.The ending is just amazing.

THE GOOD, THE BAD AND THE UGLY

Yet another classic but this one belongs to the western genre of cinema. It's directed by the great Sergio Leone.Do give it a try.

DRIVE

Directed by Nicolas Wending.It's visually pleasing to watch and the first thirty minutes of the movie is bound to get you hyped up.The movie features Ryan Gosling as the lead, a character with no name but everyone calls him 'driver' in this movie. He is...kind and that is why he is STRONG, he expects nothing from anyone.
